Who We Are

Advisory built around you, not the transaction.

Dundee M&A is a boutique mergers and acquisitions advisory firm serving business owners navigating the most important transaction of their lives. We bring institutional-quality deal experience to owners who deserve more than a cookie-cutter process.

We work with a select number of clients at any given time — so when you engage Dundee, you get our full attention, our full network, and our full commitment to getting you the outcome you deserve.

"We don't just find buyers. We find the right buyers — and we know the difference."

What sets us apart
  • Boutique attention with broad market reach across buyers, private equity, and strategic acquirers
  • Every engagement is senior-led from first conversation to closing day — backed by a dedicated team of advisors, project managers, and analysts ensuring no detail is missed
  • Deep expertise across industries including manufacturing, services, and professional firms
  • Transparent, milestone-based process from first conversation to closing day
  • Confidential marketing that protects your employees, customers, and competitive position

How It Works

A process built for clarity and confidence.

Selling a business is one of the most significant decisions you'll ever make. We make sure you understand every step before you take it.

1
Discovery

A confidential conversation to understand your goals, timeline, and what a successful outcome looks like for you.

2
Valuation

We analyze your financials and market position to establish a credible, defensible value range for your business.

3
Go to Market

Targeted, confidential outreach to qualified buyers — strategic acquirers, private equity, and family offices.

4
Close

We manage negotiations and due diligence through to a successful, fully documented closing on your behalf.

5
Legacy

The work isn't over — it's just beginning. Family wealth, philanthropy, and next generation stewardship start here.
