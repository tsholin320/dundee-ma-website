Who We Are

Advisory built around you, not the transaction.

Dundee M&A is a boutique mergers and acquisitions advisory firm serving business owners across the lower middle market. We bring institutional-quality deal experience to owners who deserve more than a cookie-cutter process.

We work with a select number of clients at any given time — so when you engage Dundee, you get our full attention, our full network, and our full commitment to getting you the outcome you deserve.

"We don't just find buyers. We find the right buyers — and we know the difference."

What sets us apart
  • Boutique attention with broad market reach across buyers, private equity, and strategic acquirers
  • Senior-led engagements — your deal is never handed off to junior staff
  • Deep expertise across industries including manufacturing, services, and professional firms
  • Transparent, milestone-based process from first conversation to closing day
  • Confidential marketing that protects your employees, customers, and competitive position

How It Works

A process built for clarity and confidence.

Selling a business is one of the most significant decisions you'll ever make. We make sure you understand every step before you take it.

1
Discovery

A confidential conversation to understand your goals, timeline, and what a successful outcome looks like for you.

2
Valuation

Travis Sholin
CEO

Travis Sholin is the CEO of the Dundee family of companies — including Dundee M&A, Dundee Family Office, Dundee Wealth Management, and Dundee Accounting. His life's work is guiding families through the full circle of wealth: from business exit and transaction advisory to legacy planning and next-generation stewardship.

A Certified Financial Planner™ with a Ph.D. from Kansas State University, an MBA from Creighton, and a Finance degree from the University of Colorado at Colorado Springs, Travis brings rare depth across the disciplines that matter most to business-owning families. He integrates M&A advisory, financial therapy, estate planning, CFO and tax strategy, and investment management into a seamless, family-centered experience.

Travis serves on the boards of the Financial Therapy Association, the UNO Banking & Finance Advisory Board, and the UNO Economics Board. A committed Rotarian and advisor to the Healing Kadi Foundation, he spent three months volunteering in Uganda and remains active in humanitarian work across Africa.

Outside of work, Travis enjoys traveling, public speaking, and spending time with his wife and five children.
